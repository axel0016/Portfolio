Robotic Tournaments

In these tournaments, participants typically use Arduino microcontrollers to program robots to complete a series of tasks or challenges. These challenges can range from navigating mazes to playing sports, such as soccer or basketball. The robots are built using a variety of materials, including metal, plastic, and even LEGO bricks, and are powered by motors, sensors, and other electronic components.
